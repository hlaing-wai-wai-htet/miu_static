Doctorate of Business Administration

Our Doctorate in Business Management programme is a four-year part-time programme designed specifically for experienced leaders in private and public sector. The DBA programme enables you to make a contribution to knowledge through the ‘novel application of theory’ rather than the ‘development of new theory’, there by differentiating it from a traditional PhD.

This programme consists of six modules. Fix modules are facilitated and the remaining one module is the research thesis. All the modules are compulsory. The first two years of the programme is spent through developing knowledge and skills in research methods, reflective practice and research leadership and governance. When you have successfully completed the facilitated modules, you progress to the research thesis. The research thesis will typically take futher two years to be completed, it might take a little more or less duration depending on your progress.

The five facilitated modules take place during the first two years. These modules are delivered through workshops, supported by online learning resources to build a strong network of peer and tutor support. The workshop schedule is negotiated with each cohort.

The research thesis takes place in the third and fourth year of the programme. You will be allocated a supervisory team, with supervision meetings being individually negotiated. You can typically expect to meet your supervisory team approximately once every eight weeks.

DOCTORATE OF BUSINESS ADMINISTRATION
  1. Principles of Research
  2. Advanced Qualitative Data Analysis
  3. Advanced Quantitative Data Analysis
  4. Developing Leadership in Business Research
  5. Reflecting on Business Practice
Fees and Admission
  • Qualification: Doctorate of Business Administration awarded by University of Northampton, UK
  • Duration: 1.5 Years + 2.5 years for dissertation
  • School Fees: 349 Lakhs
  • All tuitions fees are all inclusive, such as textbook, stration fee, assignment fee and etc.
  • Payment Structure: 1 st Month (1 st Payment): 100 Lakhs; 7 th Month (2 nd Payment): 100 Lakhs; 13 th Month (3 rd Payment): 100 Lakhs; 19 th Month (4 th Payment): 49 Lakhs
  • Type of Programme: Part-Time
Requirements

Candidates will need to meet all of the following criteria:

  • Hold a Masters Degree in business-related subject, completed within the last ten years.
  • Have a minimum of five years managerial working experience, or at least some of which should be at a senior level.
  • Be employed in a leadership / management role capable of supporting the achievement of the programme learning outcomes.
