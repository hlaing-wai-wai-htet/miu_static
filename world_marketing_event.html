Events and Activites

World Marketing Summit Myanmar 2019

Oct

21

MIU Campus

9AM - 9PM

Myanmar Imperial University had honorably participated in World Marketing Summit Myanmar 2019 as a proud academic partner which was organized by Kotler Impact and Myanmar B2B Management Magazine on 6 th October, 2019 (Sunday) from 8:00am – 4:00pm at Yangon Convection Centre.

The event had a huge turnout with 2,000 audiences participated in the event. With MIU practicing the culture of continual learning and development, the management and faculty members of MIU had actively partaken in this honorable event where greatly knowledgeable and insightful knowledge, experience and expertise had been shared among them.

Vast amount of highly valuable and insightful knowledge related to Business, Marketing and Technology has been shared by well-famed professors and entrepreneurs including globally renowned Marketing Guru, Prof. Philip Kotler, Prof. Dr. Aung Tun Thet (Chair of UNGC Network), Mr. Koji Wada (Managing Director of FUJIFILM Myanmar Limited), Dr. Sadia Kibria (CEO of Kotler Impact Inc), Prof. Dr. Marc Opresnik (Chief Research Officer & CEO of Kotler Business Program & Kotler Business Schools), Prof. Luiz Moutinho (BA, MA, PhD, FCIM) (Visiting Professor of Marketing at Suffolk Business School), Mr. Serege Pun (Chairman of Serge Pun & Associates Group) and Dr. Sai Sam Htun (Founder of Loi Hein Company).

We are very proud to be part of witnessing the event having a great succession and blissed that the event served huge beneficiaries for the continual learning culture of MIU as well as for the youths and young entrepreneurs who attended the event.
