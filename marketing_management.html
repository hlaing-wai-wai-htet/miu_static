Bachelor of Marketing Management

Our Bachelor Marketing Management Programme, will offer you the opportunity to learn about business, and prepare you for working in the business world. The course covers the core business skills that are essential in todayâ€™s work environment. After the completion of the course, the students will be able to broaden their career pathways in different Business Industries.

  • HIGH NATIONAL DIPLOMA IN BUSINESS (Marketing And Branding) (Duration: 1.5 Years) (Awarded by Pearson, UK)
    Year-1
    1. Introduction to Advertising and Marketing
    2. Business and the Business Environment
    3. Marketing Essentials
    4. Human Resource Management
    5. Management and Operations
    6. Management Accounting
    7. Managing a Successful Business Project
    8. Graphic Design Practices
    Year-12
    1. Entrepreneurship and Small Business Management
    2. Integrated Marketing Communication
    3. Product and Service Development
    4. Organizational Behavior
    5. Brand Management
    6. Digital Marketing
    7. Art Direction
    8. Research Project
    Fees and Admission
    • Qualification: Higher National Diploma in Business awarded by Pearson, UK
    • Duration: 18 Months
    • School Fees: 110 Lakhs
    • All tuitions fees are all inclusive, such as textbook, stration fee, assignment fee and etc.
    • Payment Structure: 1 st Month (1 st Payment): 20 Lakhs; 5 th Month (2 nd Payment): 35 Lakhs; 9 th Month (3 rd Payment): 35 Lakhs; 13 th Month (4 th Payment): 20 Lakhs
    • Intake: January / March / May / July / September / November
    • Type of Programme: Full-Time
    • Day and Time: Monday to Friday (9:30am to 1:00pm or 1:30pm to 5:00pm)
    Requirements
    • Matriculation Exam Passed Certificate or IGCSE, or other equivalent qualifications
    • Passport or NRIC
  • BACHELOR OF MARKETING MANAGEMENT (Duration: 1 Year)
    Year-3
    1. Business Communication
    2. Principles in Marketing Management
    3. Advertising
    4. Digital Marketing
    5. Global Marketing
    6. Research Project
    Fees and Admission
    • Qualification: BA (Hons:) Business and Management awarded by University of Northampton, UK
    • Duration: 12 Months (1 Year)
    • School Fees: 120 Lakhs
    • All tuitions fees are all inclusive, such as textbook, registration fee, assignment fee and etc.
    • Payment Structure: 1 st Month (1 st Payment): 60 Lakhs; 5 th Month (2 nd Payment): 30 Lakhs; 9 th Month (3 rd Payment): 30 Lakhs
    • Intake: March / July / November
    • Type of Programme: Full-Time
    • Day and Time: Tuesday to Friday (9:30am to 1:00pm or 1:30pm to 5:00pm)
    Requirements
    • Applications are welcomed from students with an HND (Edexcel, ABE, NCC) or Foundation Degree in Business (or overseas equivalent).
    • English Language Proficiency: IELTS 6.0 or TOEFL 550 / 213
