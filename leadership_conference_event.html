Myanmar Leadership Conference 2019

Oct

21

MIU Campus

9AM - 9PM

Myanmar Imperial University had proudly participated in Myanmar Leadership Conference 2019 as a Gold Sponsor which was organized by Myanmar Excellence Institute (MEI) on 22 nd June, 2019 – 23 rd June, 2019 at Yangon Convection Centre. Our founder, Mrs. Poht Poht Kyi had also heartily given a speech entitling, “Dreams are easy to dream but hard to fulfill them in the reality for the beloved youths, together with seven footsteps to achieve the dreams.”

This event has been led by globally renowned Marketing Guru, Dr. John C Maxwell who had shared upon his highly valuable and insightful knowledge in regards to Business, Leadership and Marketing which served as great beneficiaries for the youths and audiences who attended the event.
