MIU Donation for the Flood Outbreak

Uploaded today at 2:00 PM

Myanmar Imperial University would like to express our deepest sorrow and sympathy for the victims who had suffered from the outbreak of severe flood that occurred in Mon State, Myanmar.

On behalf of Myanmar Imperial University, our founder, Mr. Phyo Maung Maung and Mrs. Poht Poht Kyi had wholeheartedly donated a total sum of 35 Lakhs inclusive of 15 Lakhs of donation, 40 bags of rice, 2 litres of oil, 400 pieces of blankets and 100 pieces of mat for the victims of this catastrophe on 13 th August, 2019 at Kyaik Mayaw monastery.

Anyone is always warmly welcomed to be a part of our donations as every single contribution of yours means a lot and matters for many individuals and families.

If you are interested to be a part of positive contribution to our society and country, feel free to contact us at MIU Foundation and at +951 380 233-234 or +959 732 10001.
