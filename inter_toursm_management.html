Bachelor of International Tourism Management

In this programme, students will learn how to manage hotels, tour operations, cruise ships, resorts and other hospitality facilities. This programme is also a good opportunity for learners who have great interest in travel and tour industry.

  • HIGHER NATIONAL DIPLOMA IN HOSPITALITY AND TOURISM (Duration: 1.5 Years) (Awarded by Pearson, UK)
    Year-1
    1. The Contemporary Hospitality Industry
    2. Managing the Customer Experience
    3. Professional Identity and Practice
    4. The Hospitality Business Toolkit
    5. Leadership and Management for Service Industries
    6. Managing Accommodation Service
    7. Managing Conference and Event
    8. Entrepreneurship & Small Business Management
    Year-2
    1. Front Office Operation Management
    2. Hospitality Brand Management
    3. Facilities Management
    4. Hospitality Consumer Behavior and Insight
    5. Managing & Planning Event
    6. Research Project
    Fees and Admission
    • Qualification: Higher National Diploma in Business awarded by Pearson, UK
    • Duration: 18 Months
    • School Fees: 110 Lakhs
    • All tuitions fees are all inclusive, such as textbook, stration fee, assignment fee and etc.
    • Payment Structure: 1 st Month (1 st Payment): 20 Lakhs; 5 th Month (2 nd Payment): 35 Lakhs; 9 th Month (3 rd Payment): 35 Lakhs; 13 th Month (4 th Payment): 20 Lakhs
    • Intake: January / March / May / July / September / November
    • Type of Programme: Full-Time
    • Day and Time: Monday to Friday (9:30am to 1:00pm or 1:30pm to 5:00pm)
    Requirements
    • Matriculation Exam Passed Certificate or IGCSE, or other equivalent qualifications
    • Passport or NRIC
  • BACHELOR OF INTERNATIONAL HOSPITALITY MANAGEMENT (Duration: 1 Year)
    Year-3
    1. Leading Service Quality in Hospitality
    2. Live Events Management
    3. International Destination Management for Tourism and Hospitality
    4. Professional Consultancy Project
    5. Innovation in Tourism and Hospitality Management
    6. Research Project
    Fees and Admission
    • Qualification: BA (Hons:) Business and Management awarded by University of Northampton, UK
    • Duration: 12 Months (1 Year)
    • School Fees: 120 Lakhs
    • All tuitions fees are all inclusive, such as textbook, registration fee, assignment fee and etc.
    • Payment Structure: 1 st Month (1 st Payment): 60 Lakhs; 5 th Month (2 nd Payment): 30 Lakhs; 9 th Month (3 rd Payment): 30 Lakhs
    • Intake: March / July / November
    • Type of Programme: Full-Time
    • Day and Time: Tuesday to Friday (9:30am to 1:00pm or 1:30pm to 5:00pm)
    Requirements
    • Applications are welcomed from students with an HND (Edexcel, ABE, NCC) or Foundation Degree in Business (or overseas equivalent).
    • English Language Proficiency: IELTS 6.0 or TOEFL 550 / 213
