Campus Life

Student life is about experiencing Myanmar Imperial University at its fullest!

At our University, we are altogether a diverse academic community of more than 1,550 students, academic faculty, and management members from across the country and the world, living and learning on our University Campus in Yangon, Myanmar’s most populous city and most important commercial centre.

For making sure that the entire academic journey of our students is worthwhile and expands their horizons of lectures and study moments in the library, we ‘Myanmar Imperial University’ deliberately offer the most fulfilling and invaluable student experience through restlessly-conducted events and activities that are happening on campus for our students.

Students can also join the University’s clubs at the Student Service Office according to their personal interests and in-born talents for arts and sports. Our clubs include MIU Bike Club, MIU Movie Club, MIU Arts Club, MIU Reading Club, MIU Badminton Club, MIU Dance Club and many more.
